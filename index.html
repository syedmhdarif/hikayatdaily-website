FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hikayat Daily free?

Yes, Hikayat Daily is completely free to download and use. All core features including AI summaries, personalized feeds, and note-taking are available at no cost.

What platforms is Hikayat Daily available on?

Hikayat Daily is currently available on Android via Google Play Store. iOS version is coming soon.

What news sources does Hikayat Daily use?

We aggregate news from 50+ trusted sources across Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia, and global outlets, covering Business, Tech, Sports, Entertainment, and more.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and never sell your personal information. You can delete your account and all data at any time.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account directly from the app settings, or visit our account deletion page for instructions.

Does Hikayat Daily work offline?

Yes. You can save articles and notes for offline reading. Your saved content will be available even without an internet connection.
